Forging Champions Since 2010

Elite martial arts training that transforms body, mind and spirit.

Uyir Kalai National Sports Academy is dedicated to preserving and promoting India's rich martial arts heritage. We offer world-class training in six traditional disciplines — developing not just athletic ability, but warrior character.

Our coaches are nationally certified, and our students have won medals at district, state, national and international levels. We believe every student has a champion within — our mission is to bring it out.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you join us

What is the age limit to join Uyir Kalai?
+
We welcome students from age 5 years and above. We have dedicated Kids batches (5–15 years), Teen batches (15–21 years), and Adult/Women batches with no upper age limit. It's never too late to start your martial arts journey!
Is there a free trial class available?
+
Yes! We offer a free first trial class for all new students. Simply WhatsApp us at 8220118489 and let us know which program you're interested in. We'll arrange your trial at Minjur or your nearest branch.
What should I bring for my first class?
+
For the first class, just bring comfortable sportswear (track pants, t-shirt) and a water bottle. No special uniform or equipment is needed at the beginning. We will guide you on what to get after you join officially.
What are the training timings and batch locations?
+
We have Morning batches (6–8 AM) and Evening batches (5–8 PM), 6 days a week. Our main academy is in Minjur, Kanayambakam, Shetty Nagar, Chennai. We also have multiple branches — WhatsApp us for the nearest branch to your area.
Will students get certificates and medals?
+
Absolutely! Students who participate in district, state and national level competitions receive official medals, trophies, and certificates. We also provide SGFI and JASK grading certificates for Silambam students. Every achievement is celebrated here!
Is training suitable for girls and women?
+
Absolutely YES! We have a dedicated Women's Self Defense batch and girls are warmly welcome in all our programs. Many of our state and national level champions are girls. Our coaches ensure a safe, respectful and encouraging environment for everyone.
Can school students balance training with studies?
+
Yes! Martial arts actually improves focus and discipline which helps with studies. Our batches are timed for early morning and evening so they never clash with school hours. Many of our students are school and college toppers who also excel on the mat!
How can I contact or visit the academy?
+
Call or WhatsApp us at 8220118489 anytime. You can also follow us on Instagram @uyirkalai_national_silamb or fill out the contact form below — we'll reply within 24 hours. Walk-ins are also welcome during training hours!
